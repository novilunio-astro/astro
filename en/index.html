about

Astrology is the language time speaks to us

Novilunio means "new moon" in Italian — the moment a new cycle begins. Behind the project is a practicing astrologer with over 10 years of experience in natal astrology, forecasting and the destiny matrix method.

Years of training with practicing astrologers: classical Western astrology, predictive techniques and the destiny matrix. The approach is psychological, not fatalistic: your chart shows potential and cycles — the choice always remains yours.

No doom-mongering, no "retrograde panic". Every reading is done by hand — no templates, no auto-generated interpretations.
